Tourism Holdings Limited (thl) is New Zealand's premier tourism company. There are a lot of sub-brands operating under thl such as Britz, Maui, Mighty, Kiwi Experience, Waitomo Glowwarm Cave, Black Water Rafting.

Britz.cn is the first website in Chinese language in the company designed and developed by myself. It’s based on SharePoint which I have zero experience at first but finally I manage to learn and use it to achieve what we want.

Tourism Holdings Ltd. 是新西兰最大的上市旅游公司，旗下有多个旅游品牌子公司，包括Britz, Maui, Mighty, Kiwi Experience, Waitomo Glowwarm Cave, Black Water Rafting等等，而该网站是公司第一个中文网站。从2015年2月-5月，这个基于SharePoint的网站是在经理的领导下，从线框图设计，UI设计到前端建设，全部由本人独立完成的。

不同于时下许多开源框架的灵活，Sharepoint像一块硬骨头，虽然在稳定和安全性上有一定的优势，但在建设，使用和维护方面真的是非常难啃。
